Server Rules

These are the official Globalcraft server rules. Please read them carefully—ignorance is no excuse for breaking the rules.

Home
  1. Swearing is allowed
    We do not restrict swearing, but excessive harassment or targeted abuse may still result in moderation action.
  2. No Racism or Hate Speech
    Racist, sexist, homophobic, or otherwise discriminatory language or behavior is strictly prohibited.
  3. No Cheating or Hacking
    Using third-party software or modifications that give unfair advantages (e.g. x-ray, kill aura, fly hacks) is not allowed.
  4. No Griefing Near Claimed Areas
    Griefing is allowed in unclaimed areas. However, griefing or causing damage near or within claimed towns or nations is not allowed.
  5. Stealing is Allowed
    Looting and theft from unclaimed or unlocked containers is part of gameplay and is permitted.
  6. Respect Staff and Players
    Do not impersonate staff or attempt to disrupt community events. Listen to staff instructions when given.
  7. No Exploiting or Abuse of Glitches
    Do not use bugs or unintended mechanics to gain an advantage. Report any exploits immediately to staff.
